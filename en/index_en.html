UAV

The Nordic Automation UAV team is a group of individuals who bring experience for technical adaptation and operations for Unmanned Areal Vehicles. In cooperation with our team you can decide if you want to obtain a custom made UAV, need to equip your existing UAV, or want us to bring an UAV and conduct a certain task. Our team is capable of conducting the entire mission from obtaining permits to conducting works and delivering obtained data. The UAV market is an expanding area where Nordic Automation operates with certified pilots and technical experts for maximum efficiency and safety.
