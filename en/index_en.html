UAV

The Nordic Automation UAV team is a group of individuals who bring experience for technical adaptation and operations for Unmanned Areal Vehicles. In cooperation with our team you can decide if you want to obtain a custom made UAV, need to equip your existing UAV, or want us to bring an UAV and conduct a certain task. The UAV market is an expanding area where Nordic Automation operates with certified pilots and technical experts for maximum efficiency and safety.
